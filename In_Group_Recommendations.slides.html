Is in group borrowers deserve thier recomendations?¶

by Mohamed Yahia¶

Investigation Overview¶

Investigating if in group borrowers deserve thier recomendations.

Dataset Overview¶

Prosper loan dataset. It contains data of about 113937 loans and 81 features for these lonas.

Do in group borrowers have higher recomendations?¶

Yes ,in group borrowers have higher recomendations in average than out of group borrowers

They also have higher score from Prosper¶

What about delinquent days?¶

Despite in group borrowers have higher recomendations, they have higher delinquent days..

What about principal loss?¶

Lenders incure more principal loss with in group borrowers

Is this the case in all loan status?¶

In Past Due and Defualted loans there is a small difference but the problem is clear in charged off loans.
